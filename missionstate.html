Mission Statement

Vibes is a website and app that we created to help bring awareness to the blind community.

Contrary to popular belief, technology is not very accessible to those who are visually impaired due to the underdevelopment of this technology

Vibes features an app that helps the visually impaired learn braille- through vibrations

The rates of braille literacy are decreasing due to the inefficiency of printing them.

This is why we developed an app
